📐 Core Formulas

Newton's Gravity

F = G·M·m / rⁿ

Force drops with distance to power n (real universe: n=2).

Orbital Velocity

v_circ = √(G·M / r)

Closer to the star = faster orbit. 2× closer → √2 faster!

Kepler's 3rd Law

T² ∝ a³ → T = a^1.5 yrs

Period² ∝ axis³. Holds for any planet around any star.

Escape Velocity

v_esc = √(2·G·M / r) = √2 × v_circular

Minimum speed to break free of gravity permanently.

Vis-Viva Equation

v² = G·M·(2/r − 1/a)

Speed at any point of an elliptical orbit.

Angular Momentum

L = m·(x·vy − y·vx) L is conserved!

Why planets speed up near the Sun (Kepler's 2nd Law).
